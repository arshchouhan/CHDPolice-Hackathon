Email Security Tips

Check the Sender's Email

Always verify the email address of the sender. Phishing emails often use slightly misspelled domain names to trick you.

Beware of Urgent Requests

Be cautious of emails creating a sense of urgency. This is a common tactic to make you act without thinking.

Hover Before Clicking

Hover over links before clicking to see the actual URL they lead to. If it looks suspicious, don't click.
